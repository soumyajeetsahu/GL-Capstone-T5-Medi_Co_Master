Cardiology

Cardiology is medicine speciality that involves diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the heart and certain parts of the circulatory system. A doctor who specialises in this area of medicine is known as a cardiologist.

Neurology

An area of medicine that involves the diagnosis and treatment of disorders associated with the nervous system (central, peripheral, somatic, and autonomic) is called as neurology. A doctor who specialises in neurology and is trained to investigate, diagnose, and treat neurological disorders is known as a neurologist.

Dermatology

The area of medicine that deals with the diagnosis and treatment of diseases of the skin, nails, hair, and mucous membranes is known as dermatology. A dermatologist is a medical expert in the field of dermatology who can also help address cosmetic issues and aid in revitalizing the appearance of skin, hair, and nails.

Pediatrics

Paediatrics deals with the medical care of infants, children, and adolescents. A paediatrician is trained on diagnosing and treating acute as well as chronic illnesses in children. Since smaller bodies of infants and children are substantially different physiologically from that of adults, paediatricians take special care while diagnosing illnesses and prescribing medicines for them.

Ophthalmology

The medical speciality that involves the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the eyes is called ophthalmology. An ophthalmologist is a doctor who specialises in ophthalmology and can use medications, perform surgery and laser therapy, when needed, to treat eye disorders.

Frequently Asked Questions

We are here to answer all your Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do i book an online consultation?

    You can book an online consultation either on the website. Click on the ‘Doctor’ button on the homepage of the website, select a specialty or type the name of the doctor directly. Once you select a doctor, you can click on the “Consult Now’ button to start the online consultation.

  • Why do you need my personal details?

    We need your mobile number, email to ensure that you can be immediately informed when your doctor responds or when your medicines ship or to schedule your tests. We need your personal details such as - age, gender, height, weight etc. purely for medical usage by the doctor, so as to prescribe the most appropriate medicines and tests. We will never share your information with anyone else.
